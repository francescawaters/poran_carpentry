Our Carpentry Services


At Poran Carpentry, we provide a wide range of bespoke carpentry and joinery services tailored to meet your needs. Whether you're looking for custom furniture, home renovations, or unique woodwork designs, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.

What We Offer


Custom Furniture

Handcrafted furniture designed to fit your space and style. From tables to wardrobes, we create pieces that are both functional and beautiful.

Home Renovations

Transform your living space with our expert renovation services. We specialize in creating stunning interiors that reflect your personality.

From intricate carvings to modern designs, we craft unique woodwork pieces that add character and charm to any space.

Kitchen Installations

Upgrade your kitchen with our custom cabinetry and installations. We ensure a perfect blend of style and functionality.

Enhance your outdoor space with pergolas, decking, and other wooden structures designed to withstand the elements.

Repairs & Maintenance

Keep your wooden fixtures in top condition with our repair and maintenance services. We ensure durability and longevity for your investments.
